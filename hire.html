About

A world-class venue for culture and nightlife, DIAGONAL is our flagship venue. Situated in the heart of the city, this continuously evolving venue complex has brought new energy to the local area and the city's cultural scene. The site features 10,000 square metres of versatile event space providing an authentic industrial setting for live performances, concerts, exhibitions, experiential events, product launches, and conferences.

DIAGONAL has worked with iconic brands and artists, creating unforgettable experiences in our unique spaces.
